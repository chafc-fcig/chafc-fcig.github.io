Here are a number of guides, primarily related to the delivery of data.

Biological data standards tend not to be very proscriptive, in these guides we are attempting to ensure that Australian Museums adopt a consistent way of doing things. 

1. It removes a lot of decision-making from preparing data
2. It enables more effective searching across data aggregated from numerous collections.
