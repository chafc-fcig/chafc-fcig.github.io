# Faunal Collections Informatics Group

The Faunal Collections Informatics Group, FCIG, is a subcommittee of
the Council of Heads of Australian Faunal Collections (CHAFC).
 
Our primary concern is the delivery and aggregation of biological
specimen data from Australia's Museums, in particular to the Atlas of
Living Australia.
